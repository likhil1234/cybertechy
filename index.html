FAQs on Cybercrime

Cybercrime is rising rapidly in India and affects people from all walks of life. Reporting cybercrimes timely and seeking assistance is crucial. This section answers some frequently asked questions to help you take the right action.

Reporting Issues

Cybercrime refers to criminal activities carried out via the internet or through the use of computer technology. This includes hacking, identity theft, financial fraud, and online harassment.

You can file a complaint at your nearest police station or report it online on portals like cybercrime.gov.in which allow online reporting of financial frauds, hacking, data thefts etc.

Keep details like affected accounts, transaction IDs, email IDs involved, digital footprints saved to help investigations. Also share if you have details on source of attack.

Immediately update passwords, enable two-factor authentication option and check privacy settings. Report hacking attempts to social media provider.
