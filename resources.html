Resources for Cybercrime Awareness

This page provides essential resources to help you report cybercrimes and protect your online presence. Explore the links below to stay informed and safe.

Reporting Portals and Helplines

  • Cybercrime Reporting Portal: cybercrime.gov.in – Official portal for reporting cybercrimes in India, including financial fraud, hacking, and data theft.
  • Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY): meity.gov.in – Provides guidelines and updates on cybersecurity policies and initiatives.
  • Indian Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT-In): cert-in.org.in – National agency for addressing cybersecurity incidents and promoting cyber resilience.
  • Anti-Fraud Helpline (1930): Call the anti-fraud helpline number 1930 to report scams, phishing, and fraudulent transactions.

Cyber Hygiene Tools

  • Antivirus Software: Protect your devices with trusted antivirus software: Avast, Norton, Kaspersky.
  • Password Managers: Safely store and manage passwords with: LastPass, Bitwarden, 1Password.
  • VPN Services: Enhance your privacy using VPNs like: NordVPN, ExpressVPN, ProtonVPN.
  • Secure Browsers: Use privacy-focused browsers such as: Brave or Mozilla Firefox for safer browsing.
